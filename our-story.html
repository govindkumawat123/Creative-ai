About Creative AI

We believe the power of software can unlock every human’s potential

Amit Holkar

Founder & CEO

After nearly a decade in App and Product building, I and Aishwarya learned how broken the traditional development process could be missed deadlines, communication gaps, and projects drifting away from the client’s vision. The frustration wasn’t just ours; our clients felt it too : "behind every successful business, there isn’t just a great product or great people." it’s the exceptional blend of both.

That’s when the spark hit: What if building software could be as easy as ordering pizza? No delays, no miscommunication just a simple, transparent process that brings ideas to life effortlessly.

Today, that spark has become Creative AI an AI-powered platform that empowers anyone, from entrepreneurs to enterprises, to build software without the usual headaches. We’ve turned a decade of hard lessons into a mission: to simplify, democratize, and accelerate software creation for the world.

We set out to help businesses whether small businesses, startups, or enterprises turn ideas into reality. We’ve seen firsthand the impact that streamlining the development process can have in creating high-performing, scalable, and reliable products.

Since our launch, we’ve empowered over 500+ entrepreneurs and enterprises to build software using our AI-powered app builder a perfect balance of 50% AI innovation and 50% human expertise, working together to deliver world-class results.

At Creative AI, we’re not just building apps or products we’re building possibilities and lasting relationships. For us, it’s never just about delivering software; it’s about creating real value.

We created Creative AI with one mission: to make building software as easy as ordering pizza fast, simple, and hassle-free. And we can’t wait to see what you build next.

Let’s build something world-class together.

Amit Holkar Founder & CEO, Creative AI
